User Comments

   (Comment on this title)
Stick with the live show, sure this probably is more faithful to the comic but it's not worth it., 18 April 2009
Author: Darwinskid from United States

Looking back, this show was painfully annoying at times. A lot of the characters were poorly drawn and written out and the animation style was flat, not too mention a lot of the stories and jokes were just "bleh..." Just so you fans of the live action series know, this is not a prequel to the series it is a stand alone series itself, so you may notice quite a lot of changes to the concept you enjoy.

Not even the voice acting helped this show out much, though it was nice seeing both American and Canadian voice artists record on one show, bit of a rarity these days, though the original voices for Salem and Meliisa Joan Hart worked easily( Though Hart fails to really make her two characters really distinctive from each other).

If you're a fan, it's probably best to ignore this show, no it's not the worst show in the world but it's not really something to get into the hype for either.
